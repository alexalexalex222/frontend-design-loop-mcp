## Public Proof Policy

The public repo should not ship third-party site captures or ugly diff artifacts as headline proof.
Additional public proof can be either selected preview-gallery examples or whole-page before/after pairs, but it must be:
- owned/generated
- visually strong
- legally safe to publish
- aligned with the actual product story
